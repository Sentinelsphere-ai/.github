## 🔍 What is SentinelSphere AI? (The Ultimate Qualtrics Alternative)

**SentinelSphere AI** is an all-in-one, enterprise-grade research ecosystem designed to help **academics, market researchers, UX designers, HR professionals, and AI trainers** build, distribute, and analyse surveys and AI-training tasks at zero cost. 

We combine the power of **Generative AI (SENT AI)** with a robust **Qualtrics-grade toolkit** to deliver a platform that rivals expensive legacy solutions — completely free for core usage. Whether you are conducting academic research, measuring customer satisfaction (CSAT/NPS), performing employee engagement surveys, or training large language models (LLMs) with human feedback, SentinelSphere AI provides the infrastructure you need to succeed.

### 🎯 Our Core Mission
> To democratize access to high-quality research infrastructure by eliminating expensive licensing fees. We believe every researcher, student, and organization should have access to professional survey tools, AI assistance, and verified data sources without being locked into costly subscriptions.

## ✨ Key Features for Researchers (SEO & Professional Breakdown)

### 🤖 AI-Powered Survey Builder (SENT AI)
Describe your research goal in plain English, and **SENT AI** drafts a complete, ready-to-publish questionnaire in under 10 seconds. Our proprietary multi-AI failover architecture ensures the service never stalls, even during provider outages.
- **Natural Language Processing** interprets your brief instantly.
- **Automatic question-type selection** (Multiple Choice, NPS, Likert, Matrix, Conjoint, MaxDiff, and more).
- **Intelligent logic suggestions** (Skip logic, piping, display conditions, and quotas).

### 📋 620+ Expert Templates & 25+ Question Types
Skip the blank page. Choose from 620+ professionally crafted, fully customizable templates covering:
- **Customer Experience (CX):** CSAT, NPS, CES, SERVQUAL
- **Human Resources (HR):** 360-degree feedback, onboarding, exit interviews
- **Academic Research:** Consent forms, IRB compliance, longitudinal studies
- **Market Research:** Brand perception, concept testing, conjoint analysis
- **Healthcare:** Patient satisfaction, clinical feedback
- **Education:** Course evaluations, student feedback

*Supports 25+ question types including short text, paragraph, matrix, ranking, constant sum, file upload, audio/video response, and signature capture.*

### 🌍 Global Verified Participant Panel (Recruitment)
Stop struggling with participant recruitment. Tap into our **KYC-verified global participant panel** to collect high-quality, trustworthy data. 
- **Target by country, device, language, and demographics.**
- **Instant payouts** to participants upon approval (via PayPal, crypto, or bank).
- **Bot protection** via Cloudflare Turnstile and fraud detection.
- **External panel routing** with S2S postbacks, completion tracking, and fraud signals.

### 📊 Real-Time Analytics & Reporting
All analytics are included **free forever** — no hidden "Pro" add-ons.
- **Live dashboards** with real-time response tracking.
- **Cross-tabulation** and **sentiment analysis** (AI-generated summaries for open-ended responses).
- **One-click exports** to CSV, PDF, and SPSS for advanced statistical analysis in R or Python.
- **Auto-email response reports** (Pro/Premium) — server-generated PDFs delivered straight to your inbox.

### 🏷️ White-Label & Custom Branding
Establish your brand identity. Pro and Premium plans unlock:
- **Custom Vanity Domains** (`surveys.yourcompany.com`) with edge SSL.
- **CSS Studio** for full custom CSS on participant-facing pages.
- **AI Theme Generator** — describe a visual aesthetic, and SENT AI writes the CSS for you.
- **Full White-Labeling** — completely remove SentinelSphere branding from your surveys.

### 🤝 Team Collaboration & API Integrations
Run research at scale with your team.
- **Role-based access** (Admins, Editors, Viewers).
- **Researcher groups** and internal messaging.
- **REST API & Webhooks** for seamless integration with Zapier, n8n, Google Sheets, or your own backend.
- **SSO Login** (SAML/OIDC) available on Premium for secure enterprise access.
