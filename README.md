## 🚀 What is SentinelSphere AI?

**SentinelSphere AI** is a complete research ecosystem that empowers researchers to build, distribute, and analyse surveys and AI-training tasks at **zero cost**[reference:0].

We provide a **Qualtrics-grade toolkit** — completely free — including:

- **AI-powered survey builder** (SENT AI)
- **620+ expert templates**
- **25+ question types** with advanced logic
- **Real-time analytics & charts**
- **Multi-language translations**
- **White-label & custom CSS**
- **API & webhook integrations**
- **Team collaboration** (invite editors/viewers)

For **participants**, we offer flexible, paid AI-training tasks and surveys — work on your own schedule and get paid weekly via PayPal, crypto, or bank transfer.

## 🎯 For Researchers

### AI-Powered Survey Builder
Describe your research goal in plain English, and **SENT AI** drafts a complete, ready-to-edit questionnaire in seconds — with multi-AI failover so it never stalls[reference:1].

- 25+ question types (NPS, Likert, matrix, conjoint, MaxDiff, file upload, video/audio response, and more)
- Skip logic, piping, display conditions, attention checks
- 620+ expert templates across CX, HR, Market Research, Academic, Healthcare, and more

### Distribution & Participant Panel
Share your survey **for free** via:
- Public link or embed → **100% free**
- Email invitations → **free** (bring your own list)
- Social media sharing → **free**

Need a specific demographic? Tap into our **KYC-verified global panel** — you only fund the reward per completed response[reference:2].

### Analytics & Reporting
All analytics are **real-time and included free**[reference:3]:
- Live dashboards with filters and completion rates
- Cross-tabs and charts (pie, bar, line)
- AI sentiment analysis for open-ended responses
- CSV / PDF / SPSS export
- Auto-email response reports (Pro & Premium)

### Advanced Research Features
- **A/B testing** with variant surveys
- **Longitudinal studies** with automated follow-up waves
- **Reusable question bank**
- **Multi-language** translations
- **IRB & GDPR compliance tools**

### Collaboration & Integrations[reference:4]
- Invite teammates with roles and spending limits
- Researcher groups and messaging
- REST API & webhooks
- Embeddable widgets for your website
